Computer Science Department Presents
TEKLOGICA 2024-2025


We are excited to invite you to Teklogica 2024-25, a dynamic event hosted by the Computer Science Department of St. Mira's College for Girls, Pune, on 8th January 2025. This event promises a perfect blend of creativity, innovation, and fun, featuring activities such as Computer Science Events, Mathematics Poster, Statistics Presentation, Electronics Poster and Project, Computer Science Project, Mathematics Aptitude, and Non-Technical Games. Teklogica is a celebration of technology and talent, designed to inspire, engage, and showcase the skills of budding minds. Join us for an exciting and diverse day packed with learning, competition, and entertainment.We look forward to your enthusiastic participation!
