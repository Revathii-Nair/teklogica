    • Applications of matrices, vectors, and transformations in machine learning and 3D rendering.

  3. Chaos Theory: The Butterfly Effect in Mathematics
    • Understanding sensitive dependence on initial conditions and its role in simulations.

    • Applications of geometric algorithms in robotics, GIS, and computer vision

    • Visualizing truth tables, Karnaugh maps, and their role in digital systems.

Statistics Posters



Role of Artificial Intelligence in data analytics

AI revolutionizes data analytics by automating data processing, recognizing patterns, and providing predictive and prescriptive insights. It enhances decision-making through real-time analysis, anomaly detection, and personalized recommendations. AI also processes unstructured data, such as text and visuals, making analytics more versatile. Its scalability allows handling vast datasets efficiently, enabling businesses to innovate. Challenges include ensuring data quality, addressing bias, and making AI-driven insights interpretable
